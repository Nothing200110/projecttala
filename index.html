About Me

Things about me: I’m a bit shy, especially when it comes to people I care about. I’m scared of dolls, lizards, snakes, and other reptiles—but I still love them; I’m just scared. I love animals. I love writing, but I feel like I’ve lost my touch. I’m an INFP. I love Garfield. I secretly love “Sana Maulit Muli” and other Disney songs. I also enjoy ballads with a theatrical touch (please don’t judge me). I want to learn how to play the piano. I love watching classic Filipino films. I first created an Instagram account when my ex told me that Sharon Cuneta replies to her fans’ messages. I’m a fan of Sharon Cuneta. I made this because I wanted to get to know you better.

Why I Made This

To be loved is to be known. I don’t know if what I feel is something big yet, but I do know that I’d like to know you—and maybe let you know me too. That’s why I made this.
